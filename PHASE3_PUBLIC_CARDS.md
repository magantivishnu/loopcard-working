## 🧪 Test Your Public Card NOW!

### Quick Test:

1. **Find your card slug**:
   - Go to Dashboard
   - Look at your card URL (e.g., `john-doe`)

2. **Visit your public card**:
   ```
   http://localhost:3000/john-doe
   ```

3. **You should see**:
   - Your profile photo
   - Name & designation
   - Action buttons
   - Contact info
   - Social links
   - "Powered by LoopCard"

4. **Test the buttons**:
   - Click "Call" → Opens phone app
   - Click "Email" → Opens email client
   - Click "WhatsApp" → Opens WhatsApp
   - Click "Save" → Downloads vCard file
   - Click "Share" → Copies link

5. **Check analytics**:
   - Go back to Dashboard
   - Views should increase!
